Store Policies

Shipping

We offer worldwide shipping. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free over $150
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $25.00

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags and hygiene liners attached.

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at hello@summerrio.com.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We collect personal information only to process your order and improve your shopping experience. We do not sell your data to third parties.
